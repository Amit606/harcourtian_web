About Harcourtian Connect

An independent networking initiative built to unite, empower, and celebrate the global community of HBTI and HBTU Kanpur alumni and students.

Our Purpose

Why Harcourtian Connect Exists

Established in 1921, Harcourt Butler Technological Institute (now Harcourt Butler Technical University, Kanpur) has nurtured some of the finest engineers, researchers, entrepreneurs, and global leaders. With over a century of legacy, our alumni footprint stretches across multiple continents and industries.

However, the global community faced fragmented professional communication. Harcourtian Connect was launched independently as a private initiative to build a single, unified database and network that allows alumni to support one another, offer professional opportunities, and mentor current students.

Our Mission

To foster meaningful, lifelong professional and personal connections among Harcourtians, creating opportunities for career advancement, startup acceleration, and knowledge exchange.

Our Core Values

Integrity, collaboration, pay-it-forward mentorship, and supporting the technological and entrepreneurial endeavors of fellow batchmates and juniors.

Core Pillars

How We Support Our Network

Professional Networking

Connect with classmates, seniors, and batchmates filterable by graduation batch, branch, location, industry, or company. The portal makes global interactions seamless.

Mentorship & Careers

Alumni regularly share jobs, internal referrals, and career advice. Current students gain access to invaluable feedback, interview tips, and placement resources directly from senior executives.

Startup Incubation

A dedicated Business Directory designed to showcase and promote startups and business services founded by Harcourtians, offering special discount benefits within the alumni ecosystem.

Official Affiliation Disclaimer

Harcourtian Connect is an independent, private networking platform. Please note that this platform is not officially affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Harcourt Butler Technical University (HBTU/HBTI) Kanpur administrations.

All verifications, server maintenance, volunteer coordinators, and community guidelines are managed independently by platform administrators and verified batchmates to maintain network integrity.
